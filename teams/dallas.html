Dallas Fuel

Team EnVyUs is one of the oldest esports team organizations. The org is managed by Mike Rufail, who is actually listed as the Dallas Fuel owner by Blizzard, they have teams competing in Call of Duty, CS: GO, Halo, Paladins, Rocket League, Street Fighter, and League of Legends. Regarding the latter, the team have been reportedly denied a slot for the 2018 North American LCS. The very fact that the team relocated from North Carolina to Texas to found an Overwatch team seems to have been a deciding factor. Team EnVyUs received a $35 million investment from the (also Dallas-based) Hersh Interactive Group last August. The Hersh family are minority owners in the Texas Rangers MLB franchise, and were also part of esport organisation Fnatic’s recent $7 million funding round.
