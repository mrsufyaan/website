New York Excelsior

Sterling Venture Capitals and Sterling Equities, owned by Fred and Jeff Wilpon, are behind New York Excelsior. The family’s business activities mainly revolve around real estate development, especially in the Northeast United States. Fred Wilpon is a majority owner of the New York Mets, while his son Jeff serves as the franchise’s COO. Not long after it was confirmed as an OWL slot owner, Sterling Equities acquired the Korean Overwatch team LW Blue.
